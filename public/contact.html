‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Space Runner really free to play?
A: Yes! Space Runner is completely free with no hidden costs, paywalls, or in-app purchases. We display ads to support development, but the game is 100% free.
Q: Why isn't the game loading?
A: Make sure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) with JavaScript enabled. Clear your browser cache and try again. If issues persist, contact us!
Q: Can I play on mobile devices?
A: Absolutely! Space Runner is fully optimized for mobile browsers. Just visit spacerunner.fun on your mobile device and start playing.
Q: How do I save my high scores?
A: Your scores are automatically saved in your browser's local storage. Just make sure not to clear your browser data if you want to keep your scores!
Q: Will you add multiplayer mode?
A: Great question! Multiplayer is on our roadmap. We're working on global leaderboards first, with competitive multiplayer coming in future updates.
Q: How can I support Space Runner?
A: The best way to support us is to play the game, share it with friends, and provide feedback. Watching our ads (without clicking excessively) also helps!
Q: Do you collect my personal data?
A: No. We don't collect personal information. Game data stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.
