Welcome to Space Runner! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit and play our game at https://spacerunner.fun.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Space Runner is designed to be a simple, fun browser game. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to play.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • Browser Information: Browser type, version, and language preferences
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Usage Data: Game scores, gameplay sessions (stored locally in your browser)
  • Log Data: IP address, date and time of visit, pages viewed
  • Cookie Data: As described in our Cookie Policy

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Space Runner game experience
  • To improve and optimize game performance
  • To analyze usage patterns and understand player preferences
  • To detect and prevent technical issues or bugs
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your gaming experience. These include:

3.1 Essential Cookies

Required for the game to function properly, including storing game settings and preferences.

3.2 Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how players interact with the game to improve performance and features.

3.3 Advertising Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

For more details, please read our Cookie Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites
  • Measure ad effectiveness
  • Prevent fraudulent clicks

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at Google Privacy Policy.

4.2 Analytics Services

We may use third-party analytics services to help us understand how the game is used. These services may collect information about your use of the game and other websites.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Protection for Children Under 13

Space Runner does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Our game is designed to be enjoyed by players of all ages. We do not require registration or the submission of any personal information to play. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take immediate steps to delete that information.

Parents and guardians are encouraged to monitor their children's online activities. If you believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately at spacerunner.fun@gmail.com.

6. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that:

  • Most game data (scores, preferences) is stored locally in your browser
  • We do not store personal information on our servers
  • No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure
  • You play the game at your own risk

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:
