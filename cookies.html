This Cookie Policy explains how Space Runner ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website at https://spacerunner.fun.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide a better user experience.

Cookies help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferred settings, game progress, and browsing patterns.

2. How We Use Cookies

Space Runner uses cookies for several important purposes:

  • Essential Functionality: To enable core features like saving game progress and preferences
  • Performance: To analyze how the game is used and improve performance
  • Analytics: To understand player behavior and optimize the gaming experience
  • Advertising: To display relevant ads through Google AdSense

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like:

  • Storing your game settings and preferences
  • Maintaining game session data
  • Ensuring website security
  • Remembering your cookie consent choices

3.2 Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how players interact with Space Runner:

  • Which features are most popular
  • How long players typically play
  • Where technical issues occur
  • General usage statistics

This information helps us improve the game and fix bugs.

3.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your gaming experience by:

  • Remembering your high scores locally
  • Storing your preferred game settings
  • Maintaining your UI preferences

3.4 Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to:

  • Serve ads based on your interests
  • Limit the number of times you see an ad
  • Measure ad effectiveness
  • Prevent fraudulent clicks

Google AdSense Cookie Details

Google uses cookies like the DoubleClick DART cookie to serve ads based on your visit to Space Runner and other websites across the Internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, Space Runner uses browser local storage to save:

  • Your high scores and game progress
  • Game preferences and settings
  • UI customization choices

Local storage data remains on your device until you manually clear it through your browser settings.

6. How to Control Cookies

You have several options to control and manage cookies:

6.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Accept only first-party cookies
  • Delete cookies after each session
  • Set exceptions for specific websites

6.2 Browser-Specific Instructions

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

6.3 Opt-Out of Personalized Ads

To opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Visit Google Ads Settings
  • Visit Digital Advertising Alliance
  • Visit Your Online Choices (EU)

⚠️ Important Note: Blocking or deleting cookies may affect your gaming experience. Some features like saved scores and preferences may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

7. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers support a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that signals to websites you don't want to be tracked. Currently, there is no universal standard for how to respond to DNT signals.

We respect your privacy choices. If your browser sends a DNT signal, we will honor it where technically feasible.

8. Mobile Devices

If you access Space Runner from a mobile device, you can control cookies through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Safari → Block All Cookies
  • Android: Browser Settings → Site Settings → Cookies

9. Children's Privacy

Space Runner does not knowingly collect cookie data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided cookie consent, please contact us immediately.
